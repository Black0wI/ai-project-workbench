# Code Of Conduct

## Our Standard

This project is intended to be useful, professional, and welcoming. Contributors are expected to communicate with respect, assume good intent where reasonable, and focus discussions on concrete improvements to the work.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Keep feedback specific and actionable.
- Accept disagreement without personal attacks.
- Respect privacy, security boundaries, and confidential information.
- Avoid spam, harassment, and disruptive behavior.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Repeated off-topic disruption.
- Security abuse, credential harvesting, or malicious submissions.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments, issues, pull requests, or contributions that violate this code of conduct. Serious or repeated violations may result in temporary or permanent bans from participation.

## Reporting

Report violations privately to the repository owner or maintainer. Do not disclose security-sensitive incidents in public issues.
